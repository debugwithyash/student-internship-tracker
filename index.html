Welcome to the Student Internship Tracker! This application is designed to help students manage and track their internship applications effectively. With this tool, you can easily keep track of your applications, deadlines, and the status of each internship opportunity.

Key Features:

  • Application Tracking: Keep a record of all your internship applications, including the company name, position, application date, and current status.
  • Deadline Reminders: Set reminders for application deadlines to ensure you never miss an opportunity.
  • Skill Development: Access resources and tips to help you develop the skills needed to succeed in your internship applications and interviews.
  • Interview Preparation: Get access to common interview questions and best practices to help you prepare for your internship interviews.

Whether you're just starting your internship search or looking to stay organized throughout the process, the Student Internship Tracker is here to support you every step of the way. Start tracking your applications
